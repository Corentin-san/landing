About us

MMArket is a sports association which aims to promote the combat sport (especially MMA) and to bring this sport to be popular through the organization of national and international fights. And this, while allowing an unprecedented experience between professionals, followers and skeptics in order to condemn the attack on the physical, mental or moral development of minors, the non-respect of the opponent, the educational values of sport and of human dignity.
